Our story

Dallas's Trusted Painting Professionals Since 2002

To deliver exceptional painting services that enhance the beauty and value of every property we touch, with integrity, craftsmanship, and respect.

Professional Painting was founded in 2002 by a team of experienced painters who believed that quality work and honest service would build a lasting business. Starting with small residential projects in East Dallas, we grew through word-of-mouth referrals by treating every home as if it were our own. Over the years, we have painted hundreds of homes and businesses across the Dallas metroplex, earning a reputation for reliability, cleanliness, and superior finish.

Today, we continue to operate with the same values: use the best materials, prepare surfaces meticulously, and never cut corners. Our crews are trained, background-checked, and supervised on every job. We are licensed and insured, and we stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee. Whether you need a single room refreshed or a full exterior makeover, we bring the same dedication to every project.
