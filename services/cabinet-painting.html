Cabinet Painting

Give your kitchen or bathroom cabinets a fresh new look.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our cabinet painting service transforms outdated cabinets without the cost of replacement. We remove doors and hardware, clean and degrease, sand, prime, and apply multiple coats of durable cabinet paint. The result is a smooth, factory-like finish.

What's included

  • Cost-effective alternative to replacement
  • Wide range of color options
  • Durable, washable finish
  • Quick turnaround time

How it works

1

Removal & Cleaning

We remove doors, drawers, and hardware, then thoroughly clean all surfaces.

2

Sanding & Priming

We sand to promote adhesion and apply a high-quality primer.

3

Spraying & Reassembly

We spray multiple coats of paint and reinstall doors and hardware.
