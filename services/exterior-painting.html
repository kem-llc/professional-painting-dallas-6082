Exterior Painting

Protect and beautify your home's exterior with durable, weather-resistant paint.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our exterior painting service enhances curb appeal and provides long-lasting protection against Dallas sun, rain, and wind. We start with thorough cleaning and power washing, then repair any damaged surfaces. We use high-quality, weather-resistant paints and apply them with care to ensure even coverage and lasting color. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption and always cleans up completely.

What's included

  • Comprehensive pressure washing and surface repair included
  • UV-resistant, fade-proof paint for long-lasting beauty
  • Protection against moisture, mildew, and weather damage
  • Enhances home value and curb appeal

How it works

1

Inspection & Prep

We inspect the exterior, power wash surfaces, scrape loose paint, and repair any wood or stucco damage.

2

Priming & Caulking

We prime bare wood and apply caulk to seal gaps, ensuring a smooth, adherent base for paint.

3

Painting & Final Walkthrough

We apply two coats of premium exterior paint, then walk through with you to ensure your satisfaction.

Common questions

How often should I repaint my home's exterior?

Typically every 5-10 years depending on material and climate. We can assess your home's condition during the estimate.

Do you work in winter?

We paint year-round when temperatures are above 40°F. We monitor weather to ensure optimal drying conditions.
